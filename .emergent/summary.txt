<product_requirements>
The user requested a Point of Sale (POS) system named Techzone Inventory for a cellphone repair shop. The core features required were:
- **Comprehensive Inventory Management**: Add, edit, remove items, track stock levels, manage suppliers, reorder alerts, and support barcode functionality.
- **Sales & Transaction Processing**: Process sales, manage returns, apply discounts, and generate invoices/receipts.
- **Repair Tracking**: Log repair jobs, track status, assign technicians, and manage parts.
- **Customer Management**: Store customer information and repair history.
- **User Authentication**: Role-based access (Admin, Technician, Cashier).
- **Payment Processing**: Initially Stripe for card payments, with a later request for PayPal (implemented and then temporarily removed).
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Basic reports (daily sales, inventory levels).
- **Branding**: Add and customize a logo (positioning, size, cropping), company name (Techzone in blue and red), address (30 Giltress Street, Kingston 2, JA), and phone number (876-633-9251).
- **Visuals**: Integrate images for inventory items and link them to specific GSM Arena product pages.
</product_requirements>
